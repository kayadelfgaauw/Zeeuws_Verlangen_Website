Pure Indulgence

At Restaurant Zeeuws Verlangen, everything revolves around the essence of the Zeeland coast. We bring the freshness of the sea and the richness of the land together on your plate.

About Us

Nestled in a unique setting between the dunes and the polders, Zeeuws Verlangen offers an escape from the everyday. Our passion for local artisanal produce and genuine hospitality forms the foundation of every experience. Whether you join us for an elaborate dinner or a leisurely afternoon on our terrace, we ensure you feel at home from the very first moment.

What makes us unique

Local Artisanal Produce

Distinctive flavours with character Our kitchen draws inspiration from everything Zeeland has to offer: from the day's freshest catch to seasonal vegetables sourced from the region. Every dish tells a story of provenance and craft — pure, honest and prepared with love.

Hospitality

Genuine Zeeland warmth At Zeeuws Verlangen, everything revolves around warmth, attentiveness and sincere service. Our team ensures you feel welcome and valued, whether you come for a swift lunch or an elaborate dinner. Hospitality lives in our every detail.

Location

A remarkable setting between land and sea Our restaurant is situated at Resort Land & Zee, Rampweg 28 in Scharendijke. Just a stone's throw from the beach and surrounded by tranquil nature, it is the ideal place to savour fine food in a relaxed atmosphere.
